Taroudant's leisure activities are primarily concentrated within its historic medina and the areas immediately surrounding the ancient city walls. Unlike larger cities with distinct, modern neighborhoods, the main points of interest for visitors are centered on the traditional parts of the city.

### Inside the Medina (Old City)

The medina is the heart and soul of Taroudant and the most popular area for visitors. It is a dense network of winding alleyways and historical sites.

* **Place Assarag and Place 20 Ghoucht:** These two main squares are the central hubs of the medina. They are ideal for people-watching, sipping a mint tea at a cafe, and experiencing the daily life of the city. Place Assarag is often compared to a smaller, more relaxed version of Marrakech's Jemaa el-Fnaa.
* **The Souks:** The medina houses two main souks (markets) that are a must-visit. The **Arab Souk**, located along Rue Bin-Anzarane, is known for its handicrafts, spices, silver jewelry, and leather goods. The **amazigh  Souk** (or Souk Jnane al-Jaami) is a more traditional market selling fresh produce, clothes, and everyday household items.
* **The Kasbah:** The historic citadel, located in the northern part of the medina near Bab El Kasbah, was once the administrative center of the city. Today, it is a residential area with narrow streets and a very authentic atmosphere.
* **The Tanneries:** Located just outside the city walls near the Bab Targhount gate, the tanneries offer a glimpse into the traditional leather-making process.

### Outside the City Walls

The area surrounding the ramparts provides different leisure opportunities, from walks to cultural experiences.

* **The Ramparts:** The most prominent feature of Taroudant is its nearly 7.5-kilometer-long city walls. A popular activity is to walk, bike, or take a horse-drawn carriage ride along the outside of the walls, especially at sunset.
* **Gardens and Oases:** The area around the city is known for its lush palm groves and gardens, which are perfect for a relaxing stroll or a quiet picnic. The **Tiout Oasis**, located about 25 km away, is a famous day trip destination offering a tranquil setting and a traditional meal in a stunning palm grove.
* **The Palmeraie:** Just outside the walls, especially near the Kasbah, you can find palm groves where you can walk or ride a donkey to experience the rural landscape.

For a true Taroudant experience, focus your time on exploring the medina's inner life and enjoying the unique perspective offered by the city's monumental walls.
