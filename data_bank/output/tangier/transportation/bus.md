### Local Public Buses (ALSA)

ALSA operates a comprehensive bus network that covers the city of Tangier and extends to its suburbs and nearby towns.

#### Practical Info
* **Fares:** A single journey within the city is very affordable, typically costing around 5 MAD. Tickets are purchased directly from the driver upon boarding.
* **How to Ride:** Signal the bus to stop. Board and pay the driver for your ticket. When you want to get off, press the stop button before your destination.

#### Key Bus Hubs
* **Gare Ferroviaire (Train Station):** A central hub for numerous city and regional lines, including the airport and port buses.
* **Iberia:** A key interchange in the city center for many routes like L3, L4, L5, L7, and LI5.
* **Castilla:** A major hub, particularly for lines connecting to the southern parts of the region and nearby towns.

#### Key Tourist & Transit Routes
* **AE:** The dedicated airport bus service connecting the **Aéroport** to the **Gare Ferroviaire** (main train station).
* **LI3:** A vital line for travelers, connecting the **Port Tanger Med** directly with the **Gare Ferroviaire**.
* **LI2:** Connects the popular coastal town of **Asilah** with the **Castilla** hub in Tangier.
* **L5:** Runs from the city center (**Iberia**) towards the **Rmilat** park area.
