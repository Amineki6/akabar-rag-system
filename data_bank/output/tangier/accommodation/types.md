### Types of Accommodation

#### Riads & Guesthouses
For an authentic and atmospheric stay, choosing a riad or a traditional guesthouse (dar) is highly recommended. Concentrated in the Medina and the Kasbah, these restored traditional homes are built around a central courtyard and often feature beautiful tilework and rooftop terraces with stunning views of the city and the Strait of Gibraltar. They offer an intimate and culturally rich experience.

#### Hotels
Tangier boasts a wide spectrum of hotels, from legendary 5-star historic establishments that once hosted spies and celebrities, to modern, mid-range, and budget-friendly options. Luxury and business hotels are primarily located in the Ville Nouvelle (new city) and along the beachfront, offering amenities like swimming pools, spas, and conference facilities.

#### Apartments
Renting an apartment provides more space and the flexibility to self-cater, making it an excellent option for families, groups, or those planning a longer stay. Modern, furnished apartments can be found throughout the new city and in the residential areas surrounding the center.

#### Hostels
For backpackers and budget-conscious travelers, Tangier has a growing number of hostels. Often located in or near the Medina, they provide an affordable place to stay and a social environment to meet fellow travelers from around the world.
