### Gardens & Parks

* **Parc Perdicaris (Rmilat Forest)**
    A large, lush park situated on a hill overlooking the Strait of Gibraltar. It was once the property of the Greek-American diplomat Ion Perdicaris. Today, it is a public park with walking trails through eucalyptus and pine forests, offering a tranquil escape and beautiful coastal views.

* **Donabo Botanical Gardens**
    A beautifully landscaped botanical garden that provides a peaceful retreat from the city. The gardens feature a wide variety of plants and flowers from around the world.
