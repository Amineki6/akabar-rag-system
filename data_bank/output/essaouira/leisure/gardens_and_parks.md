Essaouira isn't known for its large parks and gardens like other Moroccan cities such as Marrakech (with the Jardin Majorelle) or Fès. However, it does have some pleasant green spaces that offer moments of tranquility and relaxation.

Here are the gardens and parks to discover in Essaouira:

* **Jardins d'Othello (Othello Gardens)**: This is the main green space in the city. Located just outside the medina, near the Bab Sbaa gate, these well-maintained gardens are a peaceful place for a walk, away from the hustle and bustle of the shopping streets. Shaded by palm trees, it's a popular spot for locals and visitors to relax, read a book, or simply enjoy the sea breeze. They were made famous by the filming of Orson Welles' movie "Othello."

Beyond these spaces, Essaouira's nature is mainly highlighted by its coastal landscapes: the vast sandy beaches, dunes, and trails that allow for horseback, camel, or walking tours, offering a different but equally rewarding experience as visiting urban gardens.
