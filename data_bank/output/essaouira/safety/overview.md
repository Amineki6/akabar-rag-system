Essaouira is widely considered one of the safest and most relaxed cities in Morocco, making it a popular destination for tourists, including solo travelers. Its laid-back atmosphere, manageable size, and friendly locals contribute to a positive and secure experience.

However, like any tourist destination, it's wise to be aware of your surroundings and take some basic precautions.

By being mindful of your surroundings and applying common sense, your visit to Essaouira is likely to be a very safe and enjoyable experience.
