Essaouira offers several options for medical care, ranging from a public hospital to private clinics and a network of pharmacies. Here's an overview of the healthcare facilities available in the city.

### Public Hospital

The main public hospital in Essaouira is the primary point of contact for medical emergencies and serious conditions.

* **Hôpital Provincial Sidi Mohammed Ben Abdellah**
    * **Address:** Avenue Mohammed V, Essaouira.
    * **Services:** This hospital provides a wide range of services, including an emergency department, general medicine, and specialized care. It is the facility for major medical needs and traumas.

### Private Clinics

For non-emergency issues or if you prefer private care, there are several private clinics and general practitioners in Essaouira. These are often used by both locals and tourists for consultations and minor treatments.


### Pharmacies

Pharmacies (called "Pharmacie" in French) are the most common and accessible point of contact for minor health concerns.

* **Availability**: There are numerous pharmacies throughout the medina and the new city (Ville Nouvelle). You will find them easily by looking for the green cross sign.
* **Night Service**: Pharmacies often operate on a rotating schedule for night duty (pharmacie de garde). You can usually find a list of the pharmacies on duty for the evening posted on the door of any pharmacy.

### Important Advice for Travelers

* **Travel Insurance**: It is highly recommended to have comprehensive travel insurance that covers medical emergencies and potential medical evacuation.
* **Bringing Medications**: If you have specific medical conditions, be sure to bring an adequate supply of your prescription medications, along with a doctor's letter explaining your condition and the necessary medication.

By being aware of these resources, you can feel confident that you are prepared for any health needs that may arise during your visit to Essaouira.
