Tafraout offers some local healthcare services, supported by Morocco’s ongoing efforts to expand healthcare access and improve facilities, with essential safety measures recommended for visitors and residents alike.
Local Medical Facilities in Tafraout
Tafraout is served by a local health center called مركز الصحي تافراوت (Centre de Santé Tafraout), located centrally in Tafraout city, providing general medical services and basic care. 
 Other regional health facilities are within reach, such as those in Tiznit Province and Inezgane, though distances may require transport planning.
