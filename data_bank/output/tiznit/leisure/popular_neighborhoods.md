Tiznit is a relatively small city, and its main attractions and popular areas are concentrated within and around its historic core. Unlike larger Moroccan cities with many distinct, well-known neighborhoods, Tiznit's layout is more straightforward for a visitor.

Here are the most notable areas for a visitor to explore:

### The Old Medina
This is the heart and soul of Tiznit and where most of the city's charm and historical sites are located.

* **Description:** Enclosed by a magnificent, 19th-century earthen rampart, the medina is a labyrinth of narrow alleyways and traditional buildings. It's a place where you can get lost and discover hidden gems, from artisan workshops to local cafes. The medina itself is traditionally divided into four main quarters: Id Ougfa, Aït Mohammed, Id Zkri, and Id Dalha, all centered around the historic Blue Spring.
* **Best for:** Travelers who want to immerse themselves in local culture, explore historical sites, and stay in traditional riads or guesthouses. This is also where you'll find the famous **Silver Souk**.

### Place El Mechouar
This large, central square is a key hub connecting the medina to the modern part of the city.

* **Description:** Historically used as a parade ground, today it's a bustling public space lined with cafes, restaurants, and shops. It's a popular meeting point for locals and a great place to people-watch, especially in the evenings.
* **Best for:** Experiencing the city's modern, lively atmosphere, enjoying a coffee, and having a meal. It's an easy access point to both the old medina and the newer parts of Tiznit.

### The Modern City
Located outside the medina walls, this area is characterized by wider streets, modern buildings, and a more conventional city feel.

* **Description:** This part of Tiznit is where you'll find most of the modern hotels, larger stores, and administrative buildings. While it lacks the historical charm of the medina, it offers convenience and accessibility.
* **Best for:** Travelers seeking standard hotel amenities, easy parking, and a more predictable urban environment. It's where you'll find places like Hotel Idou Tiznit.

### Coastal Area (Aglou Plage)
While not a neighborhood within Tiznit itself, the nearby coast is a popular destination for leisure.

* **Description:** Located just 15 km west of Tiznit, Aglou Plage is a coastal area with beaches and a more relaxed, seaside vibe. It's a popular spot for locals and tourists alike to escape the heat of the city.
* **Best for:** Day trips, beach activities, and enjoying fresh seafood at local restaurants. Some travelers choose to stay here to be closer to the ocean.
