## 2025 Africa Cup of Nations (AFCON) Tournament Guide

Here is a full guide to the 2025 Africa Cup of Nations (AFCON), hosted in Morocco from December 21, 2025, to January 18, 2026.



---

### Tournament Format
The tournament begins with 24 teams divided into six groups of four.

* **Group Stage:** Each team plays the other three teams in their group once (a round-robin format).
* **Knockout Qualification:** The top two teams from each group automatically qualify for the Round of 16. They will be joined by the four best-performing third-placed teams.
* **Knockout Stage:** From the Round of 16 onwards, the tournament is a straight single-elimination knockout bracket, culminating in the final on January 18, 2026.

The opening match will feature **Morocco vs Comoros** on **December 21, 2025**, at the Prince Moulay Abdellah Stadium in Rabat.
