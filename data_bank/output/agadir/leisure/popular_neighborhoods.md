### Popular Neighborhoods for Tourists

#### Agadir Bay / Secteur Touristique
This is the main tourist hub and where you'll find the majority of the large beachfront resorts and hotels. It's known for its long, sandy beach and is a great choice for those who want to be close to the sea, restaurants, and entertainment.

#### Founty
Located just south of the city center, Founty is an upscale and modern neighborhood. It has a more relaxed, European feel and is home to many apartment hotels and luxury resorts. It's a good option for those who want to be near the beach but in a slightly quieter area.

#### Talborjt
This neighborhood offers a more authentic taste of Agadir life. It was rebuilt after the 1960 earthquake and is a vibrant area with local markets, cafes, and restaurants that are often more affordable than those in the tourist zone.

#### Taghazout & Anza
Located north of the city, these are former fishing villages that have transformed into surf havens. They have a laid-back, bohemian atmosphere and are perfect for surfers, backpackers, and anyone seeking a more chilled-out, community-oriented vibe. You'll find a mix of surf houses, hostels, and guesthouses here.
