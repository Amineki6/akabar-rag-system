### Overview of Transportation in Agadir

The provided documents outline several transportation options for navigating Agadir and its surroundings. The primary international gateway is the Agadir–Al Massira Airport (AGA), located approximately 25 km from the city center.

For local travel within the city, there is an extensive public bus network operated by ALSA, offering affordable fares and specific routes to tourist destinations like Taghazout and the Kasbah. Taxis are a common mode of transport and are divided into two categories: red "Petit Taxis" for metered trips within the city limits and larger "Grand Taxis" that operate on fixed fares for longer distances, such as to the airport. The documents explicitly state that Agadir does not have a tramway system.

For travel to other Moroccan cities, the main hub is the Gare Routière d'Agadir, the city's central station for intercity buses. Renting a car is also presented as a flexible option for exploring the region, with various international and local agencies available at the airport and in the city center.
