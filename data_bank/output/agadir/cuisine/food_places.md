### Where to Eat

#### Souk El Had
This is one of the biggest souks in Morocco and a great place to experience the local food scene. You can find stalls selling traditional food, spices, and fresh produce. Recommended dishes to try here include the white fish tagine, cow feet with chickpeas, and charcoal-grilled sardines.

#### Fishing Port (Port de Pêche)
For the freshest seafood imaginable, head to the fishing port. Here you can buy the fresh catch of the day and have it grilled for you on the spot at one of the many stalls.

#### Agadir Bay
An area with a variety of luxurious cafes and restaurants. 

#### Talborjt
An area with traditional restaurants in the city center.
