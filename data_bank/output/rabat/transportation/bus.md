### Local Public Buses (ALSA)

ALSA operates an extensive and affordable public bus network in the Rabat-Salé-Temara region, connecting the city centers with suburbs and surrounding towns.

#### Practical Info
* **Fares:** Standard urban bus tickets are very affordable, typically costing around 5 MAD for a single journey. You can buy tickets directly from the driver when you board.
* **How to Ride:** Signal the driver that you wish to board. Enter and pay the driver for your ticket. When you wish to disembark, press the stop button in advance of your destination.

#### Key Bus Hubs
* **Bab Chellah:** A major hub in the city center, serving as the starting point for many urban lines, including L1, L2, L3, L4, L21, and L22.
* **Bab Had:** Another key departure point for lines heading towards Temara, Skhirat, and other southern destinations, such as L7, L8, L31, L33, and L38.
* **Gare Rabat Agdal:** A hub for the airport bus and city lines like L30.

#### Key Tourist Routes
* **AE (Airport Express):** The dedicated shuttle from **AEROPORT-GARE AGDAL**, connecting the airport with the Agdal high-speed train station.
* **L32H:** This line runs from **BAB HAD** to the beaches at **HARHOURA**.
* **L33:** Connects **BAB HAD** to the popular **PLAGE SKHIRAT**.
