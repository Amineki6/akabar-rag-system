### Parks & Gardens

* **Andalusian Gardens**: Escape the city bustle in these tranquil gardens, designed in a classic Andalusian style, located within the Kasbah des Oudaias.
* **Jardin Zoologique de Rabat**: Spend a day at the modern national zoo, which houses a wide variety of African animals in environments designed to mimic their natural habitats.
* **Les Jardins Exotiques De Bouknadel**: Wander through beautifully landscaped exotic gardens located just outside of Rabat, showcasing plants from around the world.
* **Jardin D'essais**: Relax in this botanical garden and public park.


### Gardens & Parks

* **Andalusian Gardens**
    A tranquil and beautifully landscaped garden located within the Kasbah of the Udayas. Created during the French colonial period, it is laid out in a formal Andalusian style with shaded lanes, fountains, and a variety of trees and flowering plants, offering a peaceful escape from the city.

* **Jardin Zoologique de Rabat**
    The modern national zoo of Morocco, focusing on the fauna of Africa. The animals are housed in large, open-air enclosures designed to replicate their natural habitats, including representations of the Atlas Mountains, the desert, and the savannah.

* **Les Jardins Exotiques de Bouknadel**
    Located a short drive from Rabat, these enchanting gardens were created by French horticulturalist Marcel François. They take visitors on a journey through meticulously recreated ecological landscapes from around the world, including a Chinese garden, a Brazilian jungle, and a Polynesian swamp.
