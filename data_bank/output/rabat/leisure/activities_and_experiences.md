### Modern & Urban Experiences

* **Ride the Tramway Rabat-Salé**: Experience the modern city like a local by using the efficient tramway system that connects Rabat with its sister city, Salé.
* **Grand Theatre De Rabat**: Admire the stunning, futuristic architecture of the Grand Theatre, a new cultural landmark designed by Zaha Hadid.
* **Mawazine Festival**: If you are visiting at the right time, experience one of Africa's largest music festivals, featuring major international and Moroccan artists.
* **Plage de Rabat**: Relax or surf at the city's main beach, located at the mouth of the Bou Regreg river.
* **Mega Mall**: Enjoy a modern shopping experience with international brands, restaurants, and a food court.
* **Gare de Rabat-Ville**: Appreciate the architecture of the city's main train station, a hub of modern transport.
