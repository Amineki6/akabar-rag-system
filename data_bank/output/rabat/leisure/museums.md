### Museums & Galleries

* **Museum Mohamed VI of Modern and Contemporary Art**: Discover a vast collection of Moroccan art from the 20th century to the present day in a beautiful modern building.
* **National Archaeology Museum**: Journey through Morocco's history with artifacts from prehistoric, pre-Roman, Roman, and Islamic eras.
* **Oudaia Museum**: Located within the Kasbah des Oudaias, this museum showcases a collection of traditional Moroccan jewelry, textiles, and artifacts in a former palace.
* **The Currency Museum of the Bank Al-Maghrib**: Learn about the history of Moroccan currency through an extensive collection of coins and banknotes.
* **Musée de Maroc Telecom**: Explore the history of telecommunications in Morocco at this unique museum.
* **Art Galleries**: Visit contemporary art spaces like **La Villa des Arts** and **Abla Ababou Galerie**.
* **Poupees du Monde**: See a unique collection of dolls from around the world.

### Museums

* **Museum Mohamed VI of Modern and Contemporary Art (MMVI)**
    The premier institution in Morocco dedicated to modern and contemporary art. The museum showcases a permanent collection of works by Moroccan artists from the 20th century onwards and hosts prestigious temporary exhibitions.

* **National Archaeology Museum (Musée de l'Histoire et des Civilisations)**
    This museum houses the most extensive collection of archaeological artifacts in Morocco. It covers the country's entire history, with notable collections from the prehistoric, pre-Roman, Roman, and Islamic periods, including an exceptional collection of Roman bronzes.

* **Oudaia Museum (Musée National de la Parure)**
    Housed within a 17th-century palace in the Kasbah of the Udayas, this museum is dedicated to the art of Moroccan jewelry. It features an exquisite collection of traditional Amazigh (Berber) and urban jewelry from various regions of the country.
