### Historic Sites & Kasbahs

* **Aït Benhaddou**
    A UNESCO World Heritage site, Aït Benhaddou is a breathtaking ksar (fortified village) made of traditional earthen clay. It is one of the most famous and best-preserved examples of southern Moroccan architecture and has served as a filming location for numerous Hollywood movies and TV series, including *Gladiator* and *Game of Thrones*.

* **Taourirt Kasbah**
    A vast and impressive 19th-century kasbah located within the city of Ouarzazate itself. Once a stronghold of the powerful Glaoui clan, who ruled the region, its maze of rooms, passageways, and courtyards offers a fascinating glimpse into the life of a feudal caïd (governor).

* **Kasbah of Tifoultoute**
    Another historic kasbah from the Glaoui era, located just a few kilometers outside of Ouarzazate. While smaller than Taourirt, it offers commanding views of the surrounding valley and landscape.

* **The Old Synagogue**
    Located in the old Jewish quarter (mellah) near the Taourirt Kasbah, this synagogue is a testament to the rich Jewish history of the region and is maintained by the local community.
