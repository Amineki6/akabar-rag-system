### Film & Cinema

* **Visit the Film Studios**: Ouarzazate is home to several major film studios. You can tour the sets of famous movies and TV shows at **CLA Studios Ouarzazate**.
* **Explore Cinema Museums**: Delve into the history of filmmaking in the region at the **Musée du Cinema** (Cinema Museum) and the **Museum-Theatre, Memory of Ouarzazate**.
* **See Famous Film Locations**: Visit the iconic gas station from the horror film **'The Hills Have Eyes'**, located just outside the city.

Historic Kasbahs & Sites

 Ait Ben Haddou Kasbah: Explore this magnificent UNESCO World Heritage site, a fortified ksar that has served as a backdrop for numerous epic films and series.
 Taourirt Kasbah: Wander through the maze-like passages of this impressive 19th-century fortress, once a residence of the powerful Glaoui clan.
 Kasbah of Tifoultoute: Discover this historic kasbah located just outside of Ouarzazate.
 The Old Synagogue: Visit the historic synagogue, a testament to the region's rich Jewish heritage.
 Sainte-thérèse Church, Ouarzazate: See the city's historic Christian church.



### Outdoor & Adventure

* **Drive the Tizi n'Tichka Pass**: Experience one of Morocco's most dramatic drives by traversing the high mountain pass of Tizi n'Tichka, which connects Marrakech with Ouarzazate.
* **Go-Karting & Quad Biking**: Get an adrenaline rush with desert adventures such as go-karting at **Kart Aventure** or quad biking with local operators like **Abdou Quad Aventure** and **Skoura Quad**.
* **Organized Tours**: Numerous local companies, such as **Oasis Aventure - Day Tours** and **Travels Morocco**, offer organized excursions into the surrounding desert and valleys.

### Oases & Modern Marvels

* **Palmeraie De Skoura**: Visit the beautiful and expansive Skoura Oasis, a lush palm grove famous for its many historic kasbahs and towers (**Torens Van Skoura**).
* **Ouarzazate Solar Power Station**: See one of the world's largest concentrated solar power plants, a symbol of Morocco's commitment to renewable energy.
