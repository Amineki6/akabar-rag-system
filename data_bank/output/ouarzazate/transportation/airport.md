# Ouarzazate Airport (OZZ)

## Overview

Ouarzazate Airport (`IATA: OZZ`, `ICAO: GMMZ`) is a regional airport serving the city of Ouarzazate and the surrounding Drâa-Tafilalet region of Morocco. It acts as a crucial gateway for tourists heading to the Sahara Desert, the stunning kasbahs like the UNESCO World Heritage site Aït Benhaddou, and the nearby Atlas Film Studios. While significantly smaller than Morocco's major international hubs, the airport is vital for the region's tourism and film production industries.

## Quick Facts

* **Official Name:** Ouarzazate Airport
* **IATA Code:** `OZZ`
* **ICAO Code:** `GMMZ`
* **Location:** Approximately 2 km (1.2 miles) northwest of Ouarzazate city center.
* **Operator:** ONDA (Moroccan Airports Authority)
* **Website:** [www.onda.ma](http://www.onda.ma/en/Our-Airports/Ouarzazate-Airport)

---

## Terminals

Ouarzazate Airport features **one small, single terminal building** that handles all domestic and international arrivals and departures. The facility is modest and functional, designed to process passengers efficiently. Due to the limited number of flights, the terminal is easy to navigate and generally not crowded, offering a straightforward travel experience.

---

## Airlines and Destinations

The flight schedule at Ouarzazate is limited and can be highly seasonal. The most reliable connection is the domestic flight to Casablanca, which links the region to the global flight network.

* **Primary Operator:**
    * **Royal Air Maroc Express:** Operates the essential domestic route to Casablanca (`CMN`), allowing for connections to international flights.

* **International Operators (Often Seasonal):**
    * **Ryanair:** Has historically operated seasonal routes from Spanish cities like Barcelona (`BCN`) and Madrid (`MAD`).
    * **Transavia:** May offer seasonal flights from European hubs such as Paris-Orly (`ORY`).

**Important Note:** International flight routes to Ouarzazate can vary significantly by season and year. It is crucial to check directly with airlines for the most current schedules. Most international visitors arrive by connecting through Mohammed V Airport in Casablanca.

## Passenger Services and Facilities

The services at Ouarzazate Airport are basic and reflect its size.

* **Dining and Shopping:** Expect a small café or snack bar for basic refreshments and a small shop selling newspapers, essentials, and some local souvenirs. The selection is limited compared to larger airports.
* **Financial Services:** An ATM and a currency exchange desk are typically available to serve arriving passengers.
* **Car Rental:** Several local and international car rental agencies have desks at the airport. Renting a car, particularly a 4x4, is a very popular option for visitors planning to explore the desert landscapes and mountain gorges independently.
* **Wi-Fi:** Wi-Fi may be available but might not be as robust as in larger international airports.

---

## Ground Transportation

Due to its close proximity to the city, getting from the airport to your accommodation is quick and simple.

### Taxis
* **Petit Taxis** (typically red in Ouarzazate) are readily available directly outside the terminal exit.
* The ride to most hotels in the city is very short, usually taking less than 10 minutes.
* It is advisable to agree on the fare with the driver before starting your journey, or ensure the meter is used. The cost should be very reasonable.

### Car Rental
* Picking up a pre-booked rental car at the airport is a very common and convenient choice. It provides immediate freedom to travel to the city or begin exploring the surrounding region.

### Pre-booked Transfers
* Many local hotels, guesthouses (riads), and tour operators can arrange for a driver to meet you at the airport upon arrival. This is a reliable and hassle-free option.
