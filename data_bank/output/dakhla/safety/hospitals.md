The Hassan II Regional Hospital in Dakhla is the main public health facility in the city and is considered the cornerstone of the entire health system for the Dakhla-Oued Ed-Dahab region.

### General Information

* **Full Name:** Hassan II Regional Hospital in Dakhla (in French: Hôpital Régional Hassan II de Dakhla).
* **Location:** The hospital is located in the city of Dakhla and serves the population of the city and the entire province.

### Medical Services

The Hassan II Regional Hospital provides a wide range of medical services, including:

* **Emergency Services:** The emergency department operates 24 hours a day to receive critical cases and injuries.
* **General and Specialized Consultations:** The hospital includes departments for several specialties such as general surgery, pediatrics, and obstetrics/gynecology.
* **Surgical Operations:** Various types of surgical procedures are performed within the hospital.
* **Medical Analysis and Radiology:** The hospital has an on-site laboratory for medical analysis and a medical imaging unit (radiology).
* **Inpatient Care:** The hospital has a number of beds to accommodate patients whose conditions require hospitalization.

### Its Role in the Health System

The Hassan II Regional Hospital is considered the primary health reference for the region, receiving cases transferred from rural health centers and smaller clinics in the province. It also actively participates in medical campaigns organized to provide healthcare to residents of remote areas.
