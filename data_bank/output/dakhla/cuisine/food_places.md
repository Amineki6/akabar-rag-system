Popular street foods include seafood, bissara, and msemen. Mint tea and dates are often served as a traditional welcome.
The food in Dakhla is a unique blend of flavors and traditional Moroccan dishes along with plates from around the world. Many restaurants enjoy beautiful views of the lake or farmlands.

You can enjoy a distinctive dining experience in Dakhla by visiting a selection of the best restaurants that offer fresh local dishes alongside a variety of international cuisines and comfortable cafes.
