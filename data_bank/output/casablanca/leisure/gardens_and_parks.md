### Gardens & Parks

* **Parc de la Ligue Arabe (Arab League Park)**
    The largest public park in the city, offering a sprawling green space for relaxation and recreation. With its long avenues of palm trees and formal design, it provides a tranquil oasis in the heart of Casablanca's bustling urban landscape.

* **Foret De Bouskoura**
    For a true nature escape, head to this large forest on the outskirts of Casablanca, which is a popular spot for picnicking, walking, and jogging.

* ** The main football stadium in Casablanca is the **Stade Mohammed V**. It's an iconic and historic stadium in the city and serves as the home ground for two of Morocco's biggest clubs, Wydad AC and Raja CA.

* **  There are also plans for a new, massive stadium, the **Grand Stade de Casablanca** (also referred to as Stade Hassan II), which is being developed as a host venue for the 2030 FIFA World Cup. This new stadium is planned to have a capacity of 115,000, which would make it one of the largest in the world.
