### Museums & Galleries

* **Museum of Moroccan Judaism**
    This unique museum is the only one of its kind in the Arab world. It chronicles the history and traditions of Morocco's Jewish community, showcasing a rich collection of artifacts, photographs, and ceremonial objects.

* **Musée Abderrahman Slaoui**
    A private museum featuring a wonderful collection of Moroccan art and artifacts, including posters, jewelry, and paintings collected by the late businessman Abderrahman Slaoui.

* **Villa des Arts de Casablanca**
    This beautiful Art Deco villa hosts contemporary art exhibitions featuring both Moroccan and international artists, serving as a key cultural hub in the city.
