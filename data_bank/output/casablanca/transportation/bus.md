### Local Public Buses

Casablanca's bus is the most inexpensive option for areas not covered by the tram. Official website for more details about the routes : https://www.casabus.ma/en/home

#### Practical Info
* **Fares:** A single journey costs 4 dirhams. It is best to bring a small change to pay the driver directly when you board.
* **Hours:** Buses typically operate from around 5:45 a.m. to 9:15 p.m..
* **How to Ride:** Buses stop at designated stops. However, signage is in Arabic, so it is recommended to ask the driver to let you know when to get off.
