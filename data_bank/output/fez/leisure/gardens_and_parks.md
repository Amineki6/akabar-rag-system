### Parks & Gardens

***The Jardin Jnan Sbil** is a remarkable oasis located near the Bou Inania Madrasa.The Jnan Sbil garden was created in the 18th century, during the reign of Sultan Moulay Abdallah, one of the rulers of the Alawite dynasty.

***Dar Batha Museum Gardens***The garden is in the Arab-Andalusian style: Palms, hibiscus, and jacarandas surround the splashing of the fountain.

***Jardins des Oiseaux**:Ropicana Park is a center in the suburbs of Fès. It includes a bird park and an educational farm.

***Jardin Ain Chkaf**Situated on the outskirts of the city, the Aïn Chkef forest covers 64 hectares. It represents the main source of oxygen for the city of Fès. This forest is very popular among the residents of Fès, who regularly visit to practice sports (walking, jogging, etc.).:
***Parc de L’Amérique Latine**
The Garden of the Alawites in Fez, one of the most beautiful gardens in Fez, is classified among the best tourist spots in Fez for families, and it will undoubtedly allow you to enjoy its captivating beauty.
***JARDIN LALLA AMINA**:Located in the heart of the city, on Mohammed V Avenue in front of the Rex cinema, the prestigious Lalla Amina garden was revamped in 2004. Often the venue for festivals or meetings, the Lalla Amina garden is a very pleasant place in the heart of the new city of Fez.
