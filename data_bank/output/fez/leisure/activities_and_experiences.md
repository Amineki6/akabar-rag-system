### Modern & Urban Experiences

* **The modern district of Fez is bordered by green spaces where the rivers Fez run to the north and Mehraz to the East, houses government buildings, the football stadium, the train station, the bus station, etc. There are many buildings from the colonial era: the market, the Catholic church of San Francisco, and the Spanish and French cultural centers.

* **Hassan II Avenue : where cinemas, cafes, restaurants, terraces, ice cream shops, and pastry shops are located.
* **Avenue Mohamed V has many buildings from the French era, with the central market and the Rex cinema standing out.
* **The Al Amal Theater**: in Fez is one of the most important venues in the city's cultural scene and a major player in the promotion of the performing arts in Morocco.
* **Mega Mall**: Enjoy a modern shopping experience with international brands, restaurants, and a food court.
* **Gare de fes **: The Fes train station also has beautiful architecture. A large white building catches the attention of every visitor.
