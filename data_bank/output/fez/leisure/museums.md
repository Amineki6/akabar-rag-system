### Museums & Galleries


***Dar Batha Museum: Housed in a former 19th-century palace, this museum showcases a collection of traditional Moroccan arts and crafts, including ceramics, textiles, and woodwork. The palace itself is a work of art, with a beautiful Andalusian-style garden.

*** Nejjarine Museum of Wooden Arts and Crafts: Located in a beautifully restored caravanserai (a traditional inn for merchants), this museum features intricate woodwork and a stunning central courtyard.

***Borj Nord Museum of Arms: Situated in a 16th-century fortress, this military museum offers a unique perspective on Moroccan history. It houses an impressive collection of weapons from different periods, including swords, daggers, and cannons, with some pieces dating back to the Roman and Ottoman eras. The fort also provides panoramic views of the entire Fez medina.

Other Notable Cultural Sites
***Bou Inania Madrasa: While primarily a historic Islamic school and a stunning example of Marinid architecture, the Bou Inania Madrasa functions like an open-air museum. Visitors can admire the intricate carved stucco, cedar woodwork, and beautiful zellige that adorn its walls and courtyard. It is one of the few religious buildings in Fez open to non-Muslim visitors.

***Al-Attarine Madrasa: Like the Bou Inania, this 14th-century madrasa is a breathtaking example of architectural art. Its smaller size and delicate details, including finely carved cedar and plaster, make it a memorable visit.

***Ibn Danan Synagogue: Located in the Mellah (the historic Jewish quarter), this is one of the oldest synagogues in Fez. Restored in the 1990s, it offers a glimpse into the city's rich Jewish heritage.

Art Galleries
Fez also has a growing contemporary art scene. While less numerous than the historic museums, a few galleries are worth seeking out:

***Palais Sheherazade Art Gallery: This art gallery is located within a 19th-century palace, showcasing a variety of work by national and international artists. It combines the historic ambiance of the palace with modern and contemporary art.

***Alfahd Gallery: This is a contemporary art space that promotes visual arts in various disciplines, including painting, sculpture, and photography. It also hosts cultural events and workshops.
