Chefchaouen offers a wide range of accommodation options that cater to every type of traveler, from budget backpackers to those seeking a luxurious stay. The lodging experience here is often a key part of the visit, with many places reflecting the city's unique charm and traditional architecture.

### 1. Riads and Dars (Traditional Guesthouses)

This is the most authentic and popular type of accommodation in Chefchaouen, offering a truly immersive experience.
* **Riads:** A *riad* is a traditional Moroccan house with an interior garden or courtyard. While riads are more common in larger cities, smaller versions exist in Chefchaouen.
* **Dars:** A *dar* is a smaller version of a riad, typically a traditional house built around a central courtyard. Dars are more common in the medina and offer a more intimate, family-run feel.
* **Experience:** Staying in a dar or riad allows you to live within the medina's walls. They are known for their beautiful, traditional decor, often with intricate tilework, and many feature lovely rooftop terraces with panoramic views of the city.
* **Price Range:** Prices vary widely based on luxury and location, from mid-range to high-end.

### 2. Hotels

Hotels in Chefchaouen range from small, family-run establishments to larger, more modern buildings.
* **Location:** Most hotels are located in the new town, outside the medina. This offers easier access to parking and main roads.
* **Amenities:** Hotels often provide modern amenities like elevators, on-site restaurants, and sometimes swimming pools, which are rare in the traditional medina guesthouses.
* **Price Range:** You can find both budget-friendly hotels and more expensive, comfortable options.

### 3. Hostels

For budget-conscious travelers and backpackers, hostels are a great option.
* **Experience:** Hostels in Chefchaouen are typically simple, with dorm beds and communal areas. They are a good place to meet other travelers and are often located within the medina, offering an authentic but no-frills experience.
* **Price Range:** Very budget-friendly.

### 4. Apartments and Guesthouses

A growing number of private apartments and guesthouses are available through platforms.
* **Experience:** This option provides more privacy and space, often with kitchen facilities, which is ideal for longer stays or for groups and families.
* **Price Range:** Varies significantly depending on size, location, and amenities.

### Choosing Your Accommodation

* **Inside the Medina:** If you want an immersive experience and don't mind navigating the winding alleys on foot, a riad or dar inside the medina is the perfect choice. Be aware that parking is located outside the medina walls, so you will need to walk with your luggage.
* **Outside the Medina (New Town):** If you are traveling by car and prioritize convenience and modern comforts, staying in the new town might be a better option. It's a short walk to the medina, and you'll have easier access to transportation.
