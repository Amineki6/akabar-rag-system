### Overview of Accommodation Types in Marrakech

The provided document outlines the various types of accommodation available in Marrakech. For an authentic and culturally immersive stay, visitors can choose a Riad, which is a traditional Moroccan house with an interior garden, often located within the Medina. The city also has a wide range of hotels, from budget options to world-class luxury resorts, with most modern hotels being found in the newer districts of Gueliz and Hivernage. For travelers seeking more independence, such as groups or families, renting apartments or private villas is an excellent option, offering a home-like environment and the ability to self-cater. Finally, budget-conscious travelers and backpackers can find numerous hostels and guesthouses, particularly in the Medina, which provide a social atmosphere and an affordable stay.
