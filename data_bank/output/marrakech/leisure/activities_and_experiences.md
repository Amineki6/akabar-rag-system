### Outdoor & Adventure

* **Sunrise Hot Air Balloon Ride**
    Experience breathtaking views of the desert landscape and the foothills of the Atlas Mountains with a hot air balloon flight at sunrise. The peaceful journey offers a unique perspective of the region's beauty and is often followed by a traditional Berber breakfast.

* **Quad Biking in the Palmeraie or Desert**
    For a dose of adrenaline, take a guided quad biking tour. You can ride through the vast palm grove (Palmeraie) just outside the city or venture further into the rocky Agafay Desert for a thrilling off-road experience.

* **Camel Rides**
    A classic Moroccan adventure, camel rides are offered in various locations. Enjoy a short sunset ride through the Palmeraie or embark on a longer trek as part of a day trip to the Agafay Desert.

### Popular Day Trips

* **Atlas Mountains**
    Escape the city heat with a day trip into the High Atlas Mountains. Popular excursions include visiting the **Ourika Valley** with its waterfalls, exploring traditional Berber villages, and seeing the stunning **Ouzoud Waterfalls**, the highest in North Africa.

* **Agafay Desert**
    Located less than an hour from Marrakech, the Agafay Desert offers a unique rocky desert landscape. It's an excellent option for those who don't have time for a multi-day trip to the Sahara but want to experience a desert sunset, enjoy dinner under the stars in a Berber camp, and ride camels or quad bikes.
