### Taxis in Marrakech

Taxis are a common way to get around the city. It is essential to agree on the fare or ensure the meter is used before starting your trip.

#### Petit Taxi (Small Taxi)
These are the standard taxis for trips within Marrakech's city limits.
* **Color:** Yellowish/Beige.
* **Scope:** Legally restricted to travel within the city.
* **Pricing:** All are equipped with a taxi meter, which is mandatory for calculating the fare. Insist that the driver uses it. The base fare is approximately 7 MAD. Fares are typically 50% higher after 8:00 PM.
* **Passengers:** A maximum of three passengers.

#### Grand Taxi (Large Taxi)
These larger taxis are used for longer distances, including trips to the airport and destinations outside of Marrakech.
* **Color:** Yellowish/Beige, often older Mercedes models.
* **Scope:** Authorized for suburban and inter-city trips.
* **Pricing:** They do not use meters; fares are fixed but should be negotiated and agreed upon before departure.
* **Passengers:** Can hold up to six or seven passengers.
