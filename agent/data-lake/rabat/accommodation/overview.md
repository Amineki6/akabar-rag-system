### Overview of Accommodation Types in Rabat

The provided document describes the various accommodation options available in Rabat. For an authentic and culturally immersive stay, visitors can choose a Riad, a traditional house built around a central courtyard, typically found within the Medina. The city also offers a wide range of modern hotels, from luxury to budget, which are primarily located in the downtown area (Ville Nouvelle) and are well-suited for both tourists and business travelers. For those seeking more independence or planning a longer stay, renting a private apartment or staying in an aparthotel is a popular option, with many available in neighborhoods like Agdal and Hay Riad. Finally, budget-conscious travelers and backpackers can find a selection of hostels, which are mostly located near the Medina and provide a social atmosphere.
