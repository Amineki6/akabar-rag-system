### Types of Accommodation

#### Riads & Guesthouses
For an authentic Moroccan experience, staying in a riad within the Medina is highly recommended. These traditional houses are built around a central courtyard, offering a peaceful and beautiful oasis away from the bustling streets. Many have been meticulously restored and converted into charming guesthouses or boutique hotels, providing an intimate and culturally immersive stay.

#### Hotels
Rabat has a wide selection of hotels catering to all budgets, from luxurious 5-star establishments to more modest and affordable options. You will find major international hotel chains as well as independent Moroccan hotels, particularly in the modern downtown area (Ville Nouvelle) and the upscale business districts. These are ideal for both tourists and business travelers seeking modern comforts and amenities.

#### Apartments & Aparthotels
For those planning a longer stay, traveling with family, or simply preferring more independence, renting an apartment is an excellent choice. Modern, furnished apartments are widely available, particularly in neighborhoods like Agdal and Hay Riad. Aparthotels offer a convenient middle ground, providing apartment-style living with hotel services like reception and housekeeping.

#### Hostels
Budget-conscious travelers and backpackers will find a good, albeit smaller, selection of hostels in Rabat, primarily located within or near the Medina. These offer a social atmosphere and are a great way to meet fellow travelers while exploring the capital.
