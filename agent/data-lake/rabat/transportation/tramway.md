### Rabat-Salé Tramway

The tramway is a modern, efficient, and clean public transport option that connects key areas of Rabat and Salé.

#### General Information
The network consists of two lines that intersect, making it easy to navigate between the two cities and reach major hubs, residential areas, and administrative districts.

* **Fares:** A single journey ticket costs 6 MAD. Rechargeable cards are available for frequent users, offering a more economical way to travel.
* **Hours of Operation:** Trams generally run from approximately 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM, with reduced frequency on weekends and late at night.

#### Key Routes
* **Line 1:** This line connects the Hay Karima neighborhood in Salé to the Madinat Al Irfane district in Rabat. Key stops include the **Gare de Salé** (train station), **Mohammed V - Gare de Rabat** (Rabat-Ville train station), **Bibliothèque Nationale** (National Library), and the university district of **Madinat Al Irfane**.
* **Line 2:** This line connects the Hôpital Moulay Abdellah in Salé to the Yacoub Al Mansour neighborhood in Rabat. It passes through important hubs like **Hassan II (Gare routière)** (main bus station), **Bab Chellah**, and **Médina Rabat**.
* **Connection Point:** Both lines connect at the **Bab Lamrissa**, **Pont Hassan II**, **Place du 16 Novembre**, **Tour Hassan**, and **Place Al Joulane** stations, allowing for easy transfers.
