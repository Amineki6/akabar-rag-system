Medical Facilities and Safety Information in tiznit
Tiznit offers some local healthcare services, supported by Morocco’s ongoing efforts to expand healthcare access and improve facilities, with essential safety measures recommended for visitors and residents alike.
Local Medical Facilities in Ttiznit
Tiznit  is served by a local health center called مركز الصحي تزنيت (Centre de Santé Tiznit), located centrally in Tiznit city, providing general medical services and basic care. 
 Other regional health facilities are within reach, such as those in Tiznit Province and Inezgane, though distances may require transport planning.

Hassan I Provincial Hospital: is located in the city of Tiznit and is dedicated to providing high-quality care to all patients.
