A peaceful and authentic nighttime atmosphere

Tiznit, known for its historical ramparts and vibrant medina, offers quiet evenings where locals and visitors enjoy gathering at Place Assarag, the vibrant heart of the city. In summer, it is common to find traditional cafés where one can savor mint tea while enjoying the relative coolness after the heat of the day.

Traditional Moroccan music: live performances in local cafésLocal festivals: join the nighttime celebrations in Tiznit Outings and relax by the nearby seaside. 
About fifteen kilometers away, Aglou beach is a popular spot in summer to extend the day. In the evening, you can find some restaurants and small cafés offering a relaxed atmosphere by the Atlantic Ocean, perfect for seafood lovers and for enjoying the sunset.

Strolling around Assarag square in the evening to feel the local vibe around the cafes and small shops.

Explore the medina in its alleys, where the atmosphere is cooler and the shops are sometimes open.
