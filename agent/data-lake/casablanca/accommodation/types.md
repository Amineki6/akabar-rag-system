### Types of Accommodation

#### Luxury & Business Hotels
As Morocco's primary commercial hub, Casablanca excels in providing high-end hotels geared towards business and leisure travelers. These are typically found in the city center and the upscale Anfa neighborhood, offering extensive amenities such as conference facilities, fine dining restaurants, swimming pools, and spas.

#### Apartment Hotels (Aparthotels)
Ideal for longer stays or travelers desiring more independence, apartment hotels offer a blend of hotel services with the convenience of a private apartment. Rooms are usually equipped with kitchenettes or full kitchens, living areas, and amenities like housekeeping and reception services.

#### Apartments
Renting a private apartment is a popular option for families, groups, or those wanting to experience the city like a local. This choice offers the most space and flexibility, with properties available in both modern residential neighborhoods and more traditional areas.

#### Hostels and Guesthouses
Budget travelers and backpackers will find a selection of modern hostels in Casablanca, offering both dormitory-style and private rooms. While traditional Riads are less common here than in Marrakech or Fes, there are some charming guesthouses that provide a more intimate and authentic Moroccan experience, often located in or near the Old Medina.
