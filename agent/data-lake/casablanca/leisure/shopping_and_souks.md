## A Guide to Souks and Shopping in Casablanca

Shopping in Casablanca offers a tale of two cities: the traditional, bustling souks of its historic medinas and the chic, modern boutiques and sprawling malls of its contemporary districts. This guide covers the key areas to explore for an authentic market experience or a modern retail spree.

---

### Traditional Souks & Markets

#### The Old Medina
The souks within Casablanca's Old Medina offer a raw and authentic market experience. While smaller and less tourist-focused than the markets of Marrakech or Fes, these labyrinthine alleys are packed with stalls selling everyday goods, leather products, traditional clothing, and spices. It's a place to witness daily life and find unique items in a bustling, historic setting.

#### The Habous Quarter (New Medina)
For a more relaxed and orderly traditional shopping experience, the Habous Quarter is the ideal destination. Built by the French in the 1930s, it was designed to be a "new medina" with clean, arched walkways and well-organized shops. It's renowned for its high-quality, handcrafted goods.

**Key Features:**
* Specializes in traditional Moroccan crafts, including custom-made kaftans, fine leather goods, and intricate brass and copperware.
* Home to excellent olive and spice markets, as well as renowned bakeries
* The atmosphere is calmer than the Old Medina, making it a more comfortable place to browse for souvenirs and high-quality items.

#### The Central Market (Marché Central)
Located in the city center, the Marché Central is a vibrant, open-air market that offers a feast for the senses. It's the city's primary destination for fresh produce.

**Key Features:**
* A bustling flower market at its entrance.
* The main hall is dedicated to the fresh catch of the day, with an incredible variety of fish and seafood.
* Features numerous small, open-air restaurants where you can **choose your fish and have it grilled or fried on the spot**.

### Modern Shopping

#### The Maarif District
The Maarif district, located near the iconic Twin Center, is one of Casablanca's most dynamic modern shopping areas. The streets are lined with a mix of major international brands , local Moroccan boutiques, and trendy shops. It's a vibrant neighborhood that reflects the city's cosmopolitan energy.


#### Shopping Malls
Casablanca is home to several large, modern shopping centers that offer a world-class retail experience.
* **Morocco Mall:** One of the largest malls in Africa, it houses hundreds of international brands, a large supermarket, an indoor aquarium, and an IMAX cinema.
* **Anfaplace Mall & Marina Shopping:** These malls also offer a wide selection of international stores, restaurants, and cafes, often with views of the ocean.
