### Intercity Buses

This station connects Casablanca to other cities.

#### Gare Routière Ouled Ziane (Casablanca Main Bus Station)
* **Location:** Rue Youssoufia, Casablanca, Morocco.
* **Description:** This is the main bus station in Casablanca, serving as a central hub for intercity travel to a wide range of destinations across Morocco, including popular cities like Marrakech, Agadir, and Fes. It is a major departure and arrival point for various bus companies.
* **Note:** Casablanca has several bus stops and terminals. Major companies like CTM and Supratours also have their own dedicated stations or a presence at the main bus station. It's recommended to book tickets in advance, especially for popular routes and during peak travel times. Tickets can often be booked online through third-party sites that consolidate multiple bus companies.

### Gare de Casablanca-Voyageurs (Casablanca Train Station)

* **Location:** In the Belvedere neighborhood of central Casablanca. It is well-connected to the rest of the city by taxi and Casablanca Tramway Line 1.
* **Description:** As the largest and most important railway hub in Morocco, Casa-Voyageurs is a major transit point for long-distance travel. The station underwent a major renovation and expansion, with a new, modern building that includes shops, eateries, and a first-class lounge.
* **Key Destinations:** This is the primary station for all long-distance travel in and out of Casablanca. It serves as the southern terminus for the **Al Boraq high-speed train** from Tangier. You can catch trains to all major cities from here, including:
    * Marrakech
    * Rabat
    * Tangier
    * Fez and Meknes
    * Oujda
    * Mohammed V International Airport (CMN)
* **Note:** If you are traveling to or from any major city in Morocco, your train will likely arrive or depart from Casa-Voyageurs.

### Gare de Casa-Port (Casablanca Train Station)

* **Location:** Located in the city center, near the Port of Casablanca.
* **Description:** This is the smaller of Casablanca's two main train stations. While it is not on the main North-South line, it is a convenient stop for travelers staying in the city center due to its proximity to many hotels and attractions.
* **Key Destinations:** Casa-Port primarily serves local and regional trains. You can catch the express "Train Navette Rapide" (TNR) to destinations like:
    * Rabat
    * Kenitra
    * Mohammed V International Airport (CMN)
* **Note:** While you can travel to major cities from Casa-Port, most long-distance routes require a transfer at Casa-Voyageurs.
