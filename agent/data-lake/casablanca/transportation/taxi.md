### Taxis in Casablanca

There are two types of taxis available in Casablanca for getting around the city and for longer day trips.

#### Petit Taxi (Small Taxi)
These are private taxis used for trips within the city.
* **Color:** Red.
* **Operation:** They can be hailed on main streets or found at designated taxi ranks. They operate 24 hours a day.
* **Pricing:** Short trips cost around 15 dirhams. Be sure to agree on a price before starting your journey. A 50% surcharge is applied to all rides after 8 p.m..

#### Grand Taxi (Large Taxi)
These taxis are shared and used for set routes, including trips outside the city.
* **Color:** White.
* **Type:** A minibus that offers shared rides.
* **Passengers:** Up to six passengers.
* **Scope:** They follow regular routes and can be a cheap option for day trips.
