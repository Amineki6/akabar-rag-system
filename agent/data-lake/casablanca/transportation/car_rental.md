### Renting a Car

Renting a car offers complete freedom of movement and is a good option for those who plan to explore the areas surrounding Casablanca.

#### Booking Process
Reliable international rental companies like Avis, Hertz, and Europcar are based at Mohammed V International Airport.

* **Requirements:**
    * You generally need to be at least 21 years old to rent a car and may face a surcharge if you are under 25.
    * A valid driver’s license and a credit card in the driver's name are required.

#### Expected Costs
Costs vary based on car type, rental duration, and season. Manual transmission vehicles are typically more affordable.

* **Economy:** €25 - €40 per day
* **Compact SUV:** €45 - €60 per day
* **Luxury:** €90+ per day

#### Insurance Guide
Most rentals include basic third-party liability insurance, but it often comes with a significant excess (deductible). Carefully review the coverage.

* **Common Options:**
    * **Collision Damage Waiver (CDW):** Reduces or eliminates your financial liability for damage to the rental car.
    * **Personal Accident Insurance (PAI):** Covers medical expenses for the driver and passengers. Check if your personal travel insurance already provides this.
* **Pro Tip:** Some credit cards offer rental car insurance as a benefit. Check with your card provider to see if you are covered, which could save you from buying additional insurance.

#### Driving in Casablanca
Driving in Casablanca is not recommended for the faint-of-heart as traffic can be hectic, and adherence to road rules can be inconsistent.

* Drive on the **right side of the road**.
* Be prepared for heavy traffic, especially during morning and evening rush hours.
* Stay vigilant for pedestrians and other vehicles.
