### The 2023 Earthquake

On 8 September 2023 at 23:11 DST (22:11 UTC), an earthquake with a moment magnitude of 6.9 and maximum Mercalli intensity of IX (Violent) struck Morocco's Al Haouz Province. The earthquake's epicenter was 73.4 km (45.6 mi) southwest of Marrakesh, near the town of Ighil and the Oukaïmeden ski resort in the Atlas Mountains. It occurred as a result of shallow oblique-thrust faulting beneath the mountain range. At least 2,960 deaths were reported, with most occurring outside Marrakesh. Damage was widespread, and historic landmarks in Marrakesh were destroyed. The earthquake was also felt in Spain, Portugal, and Algeria.

It is the strongest instrumentally recorded earthquake in Morocco, the deadliest in the country since the 1960 Agadir earthquake

### **Major Political Events**

* **Conference of the Parties on Climate (COP22)**
    * **When:** November 2016.
    * **What it was:** This was a landmark global event where Marrakech hosted the United Nations Climate Change Conference, attended by leaders and representatives from over 196 countries.
    * **Significance:** It served as a platform to implement the Paris Agreement on climate, adopted in 2015, and solidified Morocco's role in leading environmental and sustainable development issues in Africa.

* **Annual Meetings of the World Bank and the International Monetary Fund (IMF)**
    * **When:** October 2023.
    * **What it was:** The city hosted this massive global financial and economic event, attended by finance ministers and central bank governors from 189 countries.
    * **Significance:** This selection was a recognition of Morocco's economic role in the region and was the first time these meetings were held in Africa in 50 years.

* **Global Security Forum**
    * **What it is:** Marrakech regularly hosts this forum, which brings together security leaders and experts from around the world to discuss contemporary security challenges and counter-terrorism.
    * **Significance:** This forum reflects international confidence in Morocco's security capabilities and its role in regional stability.

### **Major Cultural Events**

* **Marrakech International Film Festival**
    * **What it is:** One of the most prominent film festivals in Africa and the Middle East.
    * **Significance:** Since its launch in 2001, the festival has attracted global stars and renowned directors like Martin Scorsese, Robert De Niro, and Sharon Stone, making it a platform for cultural exchange between Moroccan and international cinema.

* **National Festival of Popular Arts**
    * **What it is:** An ancient festival held annually that showcases Morocco's rich artistic heritage, including traditional dances, costumes, and folk music from various regions of the kingdom.
    * **Significance:** Often held in the historic El Badi Palace, this festival is a showcase for preserving intangible cultural heritage and a major destination for tourists looking to discover authentic Moroccan art.

* **Marrakech du Rire (Marrakech of Laughter)**
    * **What it is:** An international comedy festival founded by the French-Moroccan comedian Jamel Debbouze in 2011.
    * **Significance:** It is one of the most popular festivals, bringing together comedians from France, the Arab world, and Africa, and helps promote the city as a global entertainment destination.

* **1-54 Contemporary African Art Fair**
    * **What it is:** A prestigious annual art fair focused on contemporary art from the African continent.
    * **Significance:** It reinforces Marrakech's position as a leading art hub on the continent and provides a platform for African artists to showcase their work to an international audience of collectors and critics.
