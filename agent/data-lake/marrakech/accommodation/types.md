### Types of Accommodation

#### Riads
A riad is a traditional Moroccan house or palace with an interior garden or courtyard. Renting a room in a riad, or the entire property, offers an authentic and often tranquil experience, as they are typically located within the bustling Medina. Many have been beautifully restored and converted into guesthouses or boutique hotels, featuring intricate tilework, rooftop terraces, and sometimes a small pool. They provide a more intimate and culturally immersive stay compared to a standard hotel.

#### Hotels
Marrakech boasts a wide range of hotels, from budget-friendly options to some of the most luxurious in the world. You will find large, modern hotels with extensive amenities like large swimming pools, spas, and multiple restaurants primarily in the newer parts of the city, such as Gueliz and Hivernage. These are a great choice for travelers seeking modern comforts and a resort-style vacation.

#### Apartments and Villas
For those traveling in groups, with family, or simply desiring more independence, renting an apartment or a  private villa is an excellent option. This choice provides a home-like environment with the flexibility to cook your own meals. Luxury villas, particularly in areas like the Palmeraie, often come with private pools and even dedicated staff.

#### Hostels and Guesthouses
Budget-conscious travelers and backpackers will find a good selection of hostels in Marrakech, especially within the Medina. These offer a social atmosphere and are a great way to meet fellow travelers. Guesthouses, which can be similar to small, family-run riads, offer an affordable and authentic Moroccan experience.
