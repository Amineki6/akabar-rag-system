Chefchaouen's accommodation options are diverse, catering to a wide range of budgets and travel styles. The most popular and authentic choices are located within the famous blue medina.

### 1. Riads and Dars
These are the most quintessential Moroccan lodging options and are highly recommended for a truly immersive experience.
* **Riads:** A *riad* is a traditional Moroccan house built around a central courtyard or garden. In Chefchaouen, riads are typically smaller and more intimate than those found in larger cities like Marrakech. They often feature beautiful, intricate tilework, and the inner courtyard provides a peaceful escape from the bustling alleys.
* **Dars:** A *dar* is a smaller version of a riad. It's a traditional house, usually without a garden, built around a central open-air patio. Dars are more common in the medina and are often run by local families, offering a very personal and homely atmosphere.

### 2. Hostels
Chefchaouen is a popular stop for backpackers, and a variety of hostels are available, mostly located within the medina.
* **Target Audience:** Hostels are the best option for budget travelers and those looking to meet other people. They typically offer dormitory beds and private rooms at very affordable rates.
* **Vibe:** Most hostels in Chefchaouen have a social atmosphere and communal spaces where travelers can relax. They are an excellent way to connect with others and get local tips.

### 3. Hotels
Hotels in Chefchaouen range from small, family-run establishments to larger, more modern ones, often located outside the medina walls in the new town.
* **Advantages:** Hotels typically offer more standard amenities like elevators, on-site restaurants, and easier access for cars. If you are traveling with a lot of luggage or prefer modern comforts, a hotel might be a more convenient choice.
* **Location:** Many hotels are situated in the new town, a short walk from the medina, providing a quieter and more accessible experience.

### 4. Guesthouses and Apartments
A growing number of private guesthouses and apartments are available for rent, often through platforms like Airbnb.
* **Advantages:** This option is ideal for families, groups, or travelers planning a longer stay. They offer more privacy, space, and the flexibility of having a kitchen.
* **Experience:** Apartments allow you to live like a local and can provide a more independent travel experience.

No matter your choice, most accommodations in Chefchaouen, especially those within the medina, feature stunning rooftop terraces. These spaces offer breathtaking panoramic views of the blue city and the Rif Mountains, providing a perfect spot to relax and take in the scenery.
