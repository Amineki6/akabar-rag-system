### Hospitals in Agadir Area

* **Hassan II** (Regional Hospital - HR)
* **Centre d'Oncologie d'Agadir** (Regional Oncology Center - CRO)
* **Hôpital Inezgane** (Provincial/Prefectoral Hospital - HP)
* ** Soon a New University Hospital Center (CHU) in Agadir.
The future University Hospital Center, with a total capacity of 867 beds, will include a day hospital (surgical/medical day hospital, endoscopy and angiography department, nephrology department), a hospitalization center (medical and surgical specialties, Mother-Child health, emergency department and short-term hospitalization unit), 48 consultation and functional exploration rooms, a surgery center, the Regional Center oncology and the Agadir Psychiatric Hospital.
