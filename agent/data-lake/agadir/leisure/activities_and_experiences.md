### Activities & Recreation

* **Ride the Cable Car (Téléphérique)**
    Get a perfect view of Agadir from a different angle by taking a ride on Morocco's only cable car. The ride takes you from the Tildi bridge up to the historic site of the Kasbah Oufella !loc{AGD_KASBAH}, offering 360-degree views of the bay and the city below in wheelchair and stroller-friendly cabins.

* **Go Karting**
    At Morocco Racing Kart Agadir !loc{AGD_KARTING}, you can choose from three different tracks (300m, 900m, and 1200m). The facility is designed and managed by professional karting enthusiasts.

* **Sandboarding**
    A cross between surfing and snowboarding, sandboarding involves gliding down sand dunes on a special board. The dunes of Tamri !loc{AGD_TAMRI_DUNES}, located less than two hours north of Agadir, are ideal for both amateurs and professionals and offer a beautiful natural setting for picnics.

* **Lion Agadir Park**
    Agadir will soon open a park in Lions south of Agadir near the crocodile park; The park !loc{AGD_LION_PARK} will offer a safari visit to get closer to lions and observe the wild life of the Lions.

* **Explore the Beaches**
    Visit some of the best beaches in Morocco along the region's long coastline. This includes exploring nearby fishermen towns such as **Taghazout** !loc{AGD_TAGHAZOUT}, **Tamri** !loc{AGD_TAMRI}, and **Imourane** !loc{AGD_IMOURANE}, which are famous surf spots.
