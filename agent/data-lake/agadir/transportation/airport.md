# Agadir–Al Massira Airport (AGA)

## Overview

Agadir–Al Massira Airport (`IATA: AGA`, `ICAO: GMAD`) is the primary international airport serving the city of Agadir and the surrounding Souss-Massa region of southwestern Morocco. It is a key gateway for tourists visiting the popular coastal resorts and plays a vital role in the region's economy. The airport is operated by ONDA (Moroccan Airports Authority) and features a modern, single-terminal design capable of handling millions of passengers annually.

## Quick Facts

* **Official Name:** Agadir–Al Massira Airport
* **IATA Code:** `AGA`
* **ICAO Code:** `GMAD`
* **Location:** Approximately 25 km (16 miles) southeast of Agadir's city center.
* **Operator:** ONDA (Moroccan Airports Authority)
* **Runways:** One primary runway (09/27)
* **Website:** [www.onda.ma](http://www.onda.ma/en/Our-Airports/Agadir-Al-Massira-Airport)

## Terminals

The airport consists of **one main passenger terminal** which handles both domestic and international flights. The terminal is a modern, spacious building designed to accommodate the significant seasonal tourist traffic.

The layout is straightforward:
* **Ground Floor:** Arrivals hall, baggage claim, customs, car rental desks, currency exchange, and taxi stands.
* **First Floor:** Departures hall, check-in desks, security screening, passport control, boarding gates, and a large duty-free shopping area.

## Passenger Services and Facilities

The terminal is equipped with essential services for travelers.

### Dining and Shopping
* A selection of cafés and fast-food outlets are available both before and after security.
* The main duty-free shop is located in the international departures lounge, offering perfumes, cosmetics, tobacco, alcohol, and Moroccan souvenirs.
* Several smaller shops sell local handicrafts, argan oil products, and travel essentials.

### Financial Services
* **Currency Exchange:** Several "Bureau de Change" counters are located in both the arrivals and departures halls.
* **ATMs:** Cash machines are available for withdrawing Moroccan Dirhams (MAD).

### Other Services
* **Car Rental:** Desks for major international and local car rental agencies are located in the arrivals hall.
* **Wi-Fi:** Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the terminal, though it may require registration.
* **Prayer Rooms:** Dedicated prayer rooms for men and women are available.
* **Information Desk:** A central information desk is located in the main hall to assist with inquiries.

* **Grand Taxis** (large, cream-colored sedans) are the most common way to get to Agadir.
* There is a fixed-price system in place. A price board is usually displayed near the official taxi rank outside the arrivals hall. It is highly recommended to confirm the fare before starting your journey.
