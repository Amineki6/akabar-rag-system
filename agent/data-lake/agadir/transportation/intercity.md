### Intercity Buses

This station connects Agadir to other cities.


#### Gare Routière d'Agadir (Agadir Main Bus Station)
* **Location:** Avenue El Mouqaouama, Agadir, Morocco
* **Description:** The main bus station in Agadir is the central hub for regional and long-distance intercity bus services. It serves as the primary point of departure and arrival for travelers heading to various destinations across the country, such as Marrakech, Casablanca, and Tangier.
* **Note:** This station hosts several different bus companies. It is recommended to check schedules and book tickets in advance for popular routes, especially during peak travel seasons.



#### Trains
* **No Train Service:** It is important to note that **there is no train station in agadir**. The national rail network does not extend here. The nearest major train station is in Marrakech, from which you would take a bus to reach agadir.
