### Local Public Buses (ALSA)

The official ALSA website is the best source for the most current schedules and route maps: `https://www.alsa.ma/en/agadir`

#### General Information
ALSA operates an extensive and affordable public bus network in Agadir, connecting the city center with suburbs, key attractions, and nearby towns. The network extends approximately 60km north to Tamri, 50km east to Ouled Teima, and 67km south to Massa. Many buses are equipped with free Wi-Fi.

#### Practical Info
* **Fares:** Standard urban bus tickets are very affordable, typically costing around 4-5 MAD for a single journey. You can buy tickets directly from the driver when you board.
* **Cards:** For frequent users, ALSA offers rechargeable 'Ikhlas' cards and student cards for reduced prices.
* **How to Ride:** Signal the driver that you wish to board. Enter and pay the driver for your ticket. When you wish to get off, press the stop button in advance of your destination.

#### Key Bus Hubs
* **P.E. Vallée des Oiseaux:** A major hub in the city center, serving as the starting point for many urban lines.
* **Place Inezgane / Jihadia Souk Inezgane:** The primary transport hub south of Agadir, crucial for connecting to regional destinations and the airport.
* **Mouquaouama Sud Inbiaat:** A key departure point for lines heading north towards Taghazout.

#### Key Tourist Routes
* **L-AE (Airport Express):** The dedicated shuttle from **P.E VALLEE DES OISEAUX - AEROPORT MASSIRA**. A single ticket costs 50 MAD. The journey takes approximately 50 minutes.
* **Bus L32:** The main bus line for reaching the popular surf villages of Tamraght and Taghazout from Agadir (**MOUQUAOUAMA SUD INBIAT - TAGHAZOUT**).
* **Bus L24:** A specific line that goes up to the historic **KASBAH AGADIR OUFELLA**, offering great views.
* **Bus L37:** The local bus route passing near the airport, connecting it to the Inezgane bus hub (**JIHADIA SOUK INEZGANE - LAGFIFAT**). A much cheaper but less direct airport alternative.
* **Bus L16:** Connects the city center to Stade Adrar for matches or events (**P.E VALLEE DES OISEAUX- ADRAR**).
