### Overview of Accommodation Types in Tangier

The provided document describes the various accommodation options available in Tangier. For an authentic and atmospheric stay, visitors can choose a Riad or a traditional guesthouse, which are restored homes built around a central courtyard and concentrated in the Medina and the Kasbah. The city also offers a wide spectrum of hotels, from legendary 5-star historic establishments to modern budget-friendly options, with most luxury hotels located in the Ville Nouvelle (new city) and along the beachfront. For travelers seeking more space and the flexibility to self-cater, renting a private apartment is a popular choice, especially for families or those on longer stays. Finally, for backpackers and budget-conscious travelers, Tangier has a growing number of hostels, often located near the Medina, which provide an affordable and social environment.
