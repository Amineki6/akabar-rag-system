#### Intercity Buses
* **Gare Routière (Bus Station):** Ouarzazate's main bus station is the hub for all regional and long-distance services.
* **Key Operators:** CTM and Supratours are the most reliable companies, offering comfortable, air-conditioned buses to major destinations like Marrakech, Zagora, M'Hamid, and Tinghir (for the Todra Gorge). It is highly advisable to book tickets in advance, especially for the popular Marrakech route.

#### Trains
* **No Train Service:** It is important to note that **there is no train station in Ouarzazate**. The national rail network does not extend here. The nearest major train station is in Marrakech, from which you would take a bus to reach Ouarzazate.
