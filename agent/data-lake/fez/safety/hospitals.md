### Hospitals in fez

Fez has a well-developed healthcare system that includes both public and private hospitals, as well as numerous private clinics. For visitors, it's helpful to know the distinction, as private clinics often provide quicker service and have staff who are more likely to speak English or other European languages.

Here are some of the main hospitals in Fez:

Public Hospitals
*** University Hospital Center Hassan II (CHU Hassan II): This is the largest and most important public hospital in Fez and a major medical hub for the entire region. As a university teaching hospital, it offers a wide range of specialized medical and surgical services, as well as a 24/7 emergency department. It's a key facility for complex medical cases and receives patients from a large geographical area.

*** Hôpital Ghassani: This is another public hospital in Fez, providing general medical services to the local population.
