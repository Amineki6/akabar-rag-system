### Overview of fez's Emergency & Medical Services

Fez has a range of emergency and medical services available, including a public system and a growing private sector. For visitors, understanding the key services and contact numbers is essential for a safe trip.

Emergency Services and Important Numbers

In a medical emergency, you can request an ambulance by calling the "15" number, or you can go directly to the emergency department of a major hospital.

Hospitals and Medical Facilities
The medical infrastructure in Fez includes both public and private hospitals.

Public Hospitals: The primary public hospital is the University Hospital Center Hassan II (CHU Hassan II). This is a large, modern facility with a 24/7 emergency department that can handle serious and complex medical cases.

Pharmacies
Pharmacies (pharmacies) are widely available throughout Fez, especially in the Ville Nouvelle (new city) and in tourist areas.

Availability: They are well-stocked with a wide range of medications, including many that are available over-the-counter.

After-Hours Service (Pharmacie de Garde): Pharmacies operate on a rotating schedule to provide 24/7 service. A list of the pharmacies on duty for the night is posted on the door of every pharmacy.
