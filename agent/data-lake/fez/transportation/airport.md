# FEZ ( Fes Sais) Airport

## Overview

Fes-Saïss Airport (FEZ)(IATA: FEZ, ICAO: GMFF)  is an international airport located approximately 15 kilometers southwest of the city of Fes, Morocco. 

## Quick Facts

* **Official Name:** Fes-Saïss Airport
* **IATA Code:** `FEZ`
* **ICAO Code:** `GMFF`
* **Location:** fez , Morocco
* **Operator:** ONDA (Moroccan Airports Authority)
* **Website:** [www.onda.ma](http://www.onda.ma/en/Our-Airports/fes-saiss-Airport)

---

## Terminals
The airport has two terminals, with Terminal 1 being the main terminal for international flights and Terminal 2 for domestic flights. It offers a range of facilities for passengers including restaurants, cafes, duty-free shops, currency exchange, and ATMs.


---

## Airlines and Destinations

It is one of the busiest airports in Morocco, serving as a hub for several airlines including Royal Air Maroc, Ryanair, and Air Arabia Maroc.

---

## Passenger Services and Facilities

The terminal offers a range of essential services for travelers.

* **Dining and Shopping:** A selection of cafés and snack bars are available both before and after security. A duty-free shop in the international departures area offers perfumes, tobacco, alcohol, and Moroccan goods.
* **Financial Services:** Currency exchange counters and ATMs are available in the main hall.
* **Lounges:** A VIP lounge ("Pearl Lounge") is available for eligible passengers or those wishing to pay for access.
* **Car Rental:** Several international and local car rental companies have service desks located in the arrivals hall.
* **Wi-Fi:** Free Wi-Fi is accessible throughout the terminal building.

---

## Ground Transportation

Several options are available to travel from the airport in fes-saiss  to the city center of fez.

### Airport Bus
* **AE (Airport Express):*The airport Fez Saiss is located about 15 km from the city center of Fez. The bus (number 16) offers regular trips between the airport and the bus and train stations of Fez. The trip takes 40 mins from the airport to the central train station. Taxis are available for hire at the exit of the passenger terminal. The rate to the city centre is fixed at 150 dirhams.

* The bus schedule is generally aligned with flight arrivals and departures. This is often the most convenient public transport option.

### Taxis
* **Grand Taxis** are available 24/7 from the official taxi rank located outside the arrivals hall.
* Taxi is a good transportation option to get from FEZ airport to your destination. The journey time to the city center is 25 minutes.


### Car Rental
* Renting a car is a practical option for those who plan to explore the area or travel independently between cities. The rental agency counters are easy to find upon exiting the baggage claim area.
