Essaouira, known as the "Pearl of the Atlantic," offers a wide range of accommodation options to suit every budget and travel style. Whether you are looking for a cultural immersion, a relaxing beach stay, or a peaceful retreat, you will find a perfect place to stay.

---

### Types of Accommodation

* **Riads and Guesthouses**: This is the most authentic option for staying in Essaouira. These traditional homes, located primarily within the medina, are built around a central courtyard. They offer an intimate atmosphere, personalized service, and a refined local decor. They are perfect for those seeking a total immersion in the local culture.

* **Hostels**: Ideal for budget travelers, backpackers, and those who want to meet other people. Located in the medina or near the beach, these hostels offer dormitories or private rooms at very affordable prices. They are known for their friendly atmosphere and lively common areas.

* **Hotels**: Found mostly outside the medina's ramparts near the beach, hotels in Essaouira offer more traditional services such as swimming pools, restaurants, and spas. You can find establishments of all categories, from modest beach hotels to luxury resorts with golf courses and thalassotherapy centers.

* **Villas and Vacation Rentals**: For groups, families, or those who want more privacy, renting a villa or an apartment is an excellent solution. Many options are available both in the city and in the surrounding countryside, often offering a peaceful setting with a pool and a garden, perfect for a tranquil escape.

---

### Where to Stay: The Best Neighborhoods

* **The Medina**: This is the historic heart of the city. Staying here immerses you in the bustle of the souks, the sounds of the port, and the unique architecture. It's the perfect place to explore the winding alleys on foot, discover artisan shops, and enjoy the authentic atmosphere of Essaouira.

* **The Beach Area**: Located just outside the medina's walls, this neighborhood is ideal for water sports enthusiasts (windsurfing, kitesurfing) and those who want to enjoy the sun. This is where most of the large hotels and modern beachfront restaurants are located.

* **The Countryside**: A few kilometers outside Essaouira, rural villages offer a retreat away from the hustle and bustle. Rural guesthouses and villas are an option for those seeking serenity and who want to discover the local life of the surrounding areas.
