Taroudant, while known for its historic walls and bustling souks, also offers a number of tranquil green spaces and gardens that provide a peaceful escape from the city's vibrant atmosphere. These areas are ideal for relaxation, a quiet stroll, or enjoying the natural beauty of the Souss Valley.

### Gardens and Green Spaces within the City

* **Jardin du Riad Taroudant:** Many of the city's beautiful riads and guesthouses are centered around lush inner gardens. These private gardens, often filled with citrus trees, bougainvillea, and fragrant jasmine, are a key feature of the accommodation and offer a quiet sanctuary for guests.
* **Public Gardens and Squares:** While not extensive parks, the public squares like **Place Assarag** are adorned with palm trees and flowers, providing a pleasant setting to sit and people-watch. The city's main avenues also feature rows of trees and some small, well-maintained green spaces.

### Gardens and Oases Around Taroudant

The true natural beauty and leisure opportunities lie just outside the city walls in the surrounding landscape.

* **The Palmeraie (Palm Grove):** The area immediately surrounding the city is known for its extensive palm groves. Taking a walk, riding a donkey, or even a horse-drawn carriage through the **palmeraie** is a popular leisure activity. These groves offer a tranquil, shaded environment perfect for a leisurely afternoon.
* **Tiout Oasis:** Located about 25 kilometers from Taroudant, the Tiout Oasis is a spectacular and popular day-trip destination. This lush palm grove is situated at the foot of a stunning kasbah that offers panoramic views of the entire oasis. Visitors can explore the oasis on foot or by a guided donkey ride through the palm trees and ancient irrigation channels. Many tours include a traditional lunch at a local restaurant overlooking the grove.
* **Afgou Oasis:** Further into the Anti-Atlas Mountains, the Afgou Oasis offers another beautiful natural escape. This is a more rugged, less-visited location, ideal for those who enjoy hiking and discovering remote natural landscapes.

While Taroudant may not have large, formal public parks like those found in major metropolitan cities, its charm lies in its intimate riad gardens and the vast, natural oases that surround it, providing unique opportunities for relaxation and exploration.
