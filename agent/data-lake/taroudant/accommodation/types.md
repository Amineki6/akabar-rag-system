Taroudant offers a range of accommodation types that provide a more authentic and intimate experience compared to larger tourist cities. The most popular choices reflect the city's traditional character.

### Riads and Dars (Traditional Guesthouses)
This is the most common and sought-after form of accommodation in Taroudant. A riad is a traditional Moroccan house with a central courtyard or garden, while a dar is a similar but smaller version. They are typically located within the ancient city walls, providing a peaceful and immersive experience. Riads are known for their beautiful architecture, intricate tilework, and serene atmosphere. They are often family-run, offering personalized service and a true sense of Moroccan hospitality.

### Hotels
Taroudant has a variety of hotels, from simple budget options to more luxurious establishments. These can be found both inside the medina and in the areas just outside the city. Larger hotels often provide modern amenities such as swimming pools, spas, and restaurants, catering to travelers who prefer a more conventional stay.

### Kasbahs
For a unique and historically-rich experience, some accommodations are built in the style of a traditional kasbah—a fortified house or citadel. These are often located on the outskirts of the city, surrounded by lush gardens or palm groves. Staying in a kasbah provides a sense of seclusion and a glimpse into the region's historical architecture.

### Guesthouses (Maisons d'Hôtes)
Similar to riads, guesthouses offer a more personal and homely atmosphere. They can be found in the city and the surrounding countryside, including the nearby palm oases. These options are ideal for travelers looking for a quiet retreat and an opportunity to experience the local, rural lifestyle.

Overall, the accommodation in Taroudant is known for its peaceful and tranquil atmosphere. Many establishments are designed to be sanctuaries from the outside world, with inner courtyards and rooftop terraces that are perfect for relaxing. For an authentic and memorable stay, choosing a riad or a traditional guesthouse is highly recommended.
