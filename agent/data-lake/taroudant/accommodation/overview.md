Taroudant offers a range of accommodation options that cater to different preferences and budgets, but the most popular choices are those that reflect the city's traditional and authentic character. Staying in Taroudant provides a more intimate and less commercialized experience compared to larger tourist cities.

### Types of Accommodation

* **Riads and Dars (Traditional Guesthouses):** This is arguably the most popular and authentic way to stay in Taroudant. A riad is a traditional Moroccan house with an inner courtyard or garden, while a dar is a smaller version of this. These guesthouses are often located within the old city walls, providing a quiet, charming, and immersive experience. They are typically family-run, beautifully decorated with local handicrafts, and offer personalized service.
* **Hotels:** Taroudant has a selection of hotels ranging from budget-friendly options to more luxurious establishments. These can be found both inside the medina and on the outskirts of the city. Larger hotels often feature modern amenities such as swimming pools, restaurants, and bars.
* **Kasbahs:** For a truly unique experience, some travelers choose to stay in a traditional kasbah or a hotel built in the style of one. These fortified residences offer a glimpse into the region's historical architecture and are often set amidst beautiful gardens, providing a sense of tranquility and seclusion.
* **Guesthouses (Maisons d'Hôtes):** Similar to riads, guesthouses offer a more personal and homely atmosphere. They can be found both within the city and in the surrounding areas, including palm groves and villages, offering a chance to experience the countryside.

### Price and Atmosphere

* **Budget-Friendly:** You can find affordable rooms in smaller guesthouses or simple hotels. These accommodations are clean and provide all the basic amenities needed for a comfortable stay.
* **Mid-Range:** Most of Taroudant's riads and dars fall into this category. They offer excellent value, combining traditional charm with a high level of comfort and service. This is the ideal option for travelers looking to experience the local culture without a high price tag.
* **Luxury:** Taroudant also has a few upscale options, including luxurious riads and hotels with elaborate gardens, gourmet restaurants, and spas. These accommodations provide a high-end, relaxing escape.

Overall, the accommodation in Taroudant is known for its quiet and peaceful atmosphere. Many establishments are designed to be sanctuaries from the outside world, with inner courtyards and rooftop terraces perfect for relaxing. For an authentic and memorable stay, choosing a riad or a traditional guesthouse within the medina is highly recommended.
